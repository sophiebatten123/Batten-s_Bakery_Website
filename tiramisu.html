Authentic Italian Tiramisu

Ingredients

315g plain flour

3⁄4 tsp baking powder

1 3⁄4 tsp baking powder

250ml water

150ml sunflower oil

180g caster sugar

1 tbsp vanilla extract

1 tbsp almond extract

80ml almond milk

250g firm silken tofu

1h 35m

Serves 12

Gluten Free

Method

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180°. Grease a 23 x 33cm / 9 x 13 in rectangular cake tin and line it with baking parchment.


Step 2

In a large bowl sift together the plain flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt. In a jug whisk together the caster sugar, oil, vinegar, water, vanilla extract and almond extract.


Step 3

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and whisk until they are just combined. Don’t over-beat the batter as that can make the cake tough. Pour the batter into the prepared tin and spread it level. Bake for about 20 minutes until it is golden and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.


Step 4

To make the cream, soak the cashews in boiling water for at least half an hour to soften them. Drain them well and place in a blender or food processor with the silken tofu, lemon juice, melted refined coconut oil, non-dairy milk, caster sugar, vanilla extract and salt.


Step 5

To assemble the tiramisu, mix together the cooled very strong black coffee or espresso, and your choice of alcohol. Pour it into a shallow bowl that is wide enough to dunk the cake fingers into.
