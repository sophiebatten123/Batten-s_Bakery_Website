Toffee Crisp Croffle Cake

Ingredients

200g dairy free butter

2 tbsp gluten free bread flour

2 tsp whole psyllium husks

1 tsp baking powder

240ml almond milk

2 large eggs

250g caster sugar

1h 5m

Serves 10

Vegan

Method

Step 1

Preheat the waffle maker to medium heat.


Step 2

Heat the oven to 180°. Line and butter two 18cm sandwich tins. Add the sugar and butter to a bowl and whisk until very fluffy and pale.


Step 3

Whisk the eggs in a mug with a fork, then add them gradually to the mixture with 1 tbsp of flour each time. (Make sure you don't use all the flour.) When the eggs have been fully combined into the mix, add the rest of the flour and fold it in gently.


Step 4

Add the dissolved toffee to the mixture, still folding. Divide into the waffle maker and cook for 25-30 min until risen. Leave to cool for 5 mins then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. Can be frozen at this stage.


Step 5

Spread fresh toffee over the top of your croffle cakes to taste.
